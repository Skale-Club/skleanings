<objective>
## Goal
Replace the two separate "Users" and "Staff" sidebar items with a single "Users" section that contains both types — platform users (admin accounts) and staff members (cleaning professionals) — in an internal tabbed interface.

## Purpose
Currently admins see two separate nav items for managing people. A user is a base entity that may or may not be an admin, and may or may not be a staff member. Merging them into one "Users" section removes the confusion and gives a unified people-management experience.

## Output
- `client/src/components/admin/UnifiedUsersSection.tsx` — new component with two tabs: "Users" (wraps existing UsersSection) and "Staff" (wraps existing StaffSection)
- `client/src/pages/Admin.tsx` — remove "Staff" nav item; use UnifiedUsersSection for "users"; remove unused imports
- `client/src/components/admin/shared/types.ts` — remove `'staff'` from AdminSection union type
</objective>
