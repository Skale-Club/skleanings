<objective>
## Goal
After login, redirect users based on their role: admin/user → /admin, staff → /staff/settings. Protect /admin routes so staff role gets redirected to /staff/settings. Add /staff/* route group in App.tsx with a placeholder page.

## Purpose
Without this, a staff member logging in would land on the full admin dashboard they shouldn't see. This plan establishes the role-based routing foundation.

## Output
- `AdminLogin.tsx` — redirects by role after auth
- `App.tsx` — adds /staff/* route group
- `Admin.tsx` — guards against staff role, redirects to /staff/settings
</objective>
